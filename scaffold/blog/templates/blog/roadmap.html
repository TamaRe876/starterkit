Roadmap

MMC NFT Platform.

Introduction:

Metaverse Music Company (MMC) is a Binance Smart Chain (BSC) BEP-20 token that provides artists with a platform to mint NFTs of their music and art. This white paper outlines the purpose of MMC, its technical specifications, and how it plans to achieve its objectives.

Tokenomics:

The Metaverse Music Company token, MMC, is a BSC BEP-20 token. The total supply of MMC tokens is 1,000,000,000, of which 20% is allocated for the initial coin offering (ICO), 30% for platform development and maintenance, 20% for marketing, 15% for team members, and 15% for advisors.

Purpose:

MMC is designed to provide artists with a platform to mint NFTs of their music and art. This platform aims to revolutionize the music and art industry by providing a decentralized platform where artists can create, mint, and sell their NFTs directly to their fans. The platform will allow artists to create unique NFTs, giving their fans the opportunity to own a piece of their favorite artists' music or artwork. This will create a new revenue stream for artists and allow them to connect with their fans in a new way. Fans will have the opportunity to own a unique piece of art or music and support their favorite artists directly.

Technology:

MMC is built on the Binance Smart Chain, a blockchain platform that is known for its fast transaction speed and low transaction fees. The Binance Smart Chain is also compatible with Ethereum, which means that MMC can be used on any platform that supports Ethereum. The platform uses smart contracts to ensure that the NFTs created by the artists are unique and cannot be duplicated. The smart contract also ensures that the ownership of the NFT is transferred to the buyer securely. The platform will be accessible through a website that is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate. Artists will be able to upload their music or art, mint their NFTs, and set the price for their NFTs.

Marketing:

The marketing strategy for MMC is focused on building a community of artists and fans. The team plans to use social media, influencer marketing, and partnerships with other platforms to attract artists and fans to the platform. The team also plans to organize events, such as concerts and art shows, to showcase the work of artists who use the platform. These events will create awareness of the platform and attract more artists and fans to the platform.
